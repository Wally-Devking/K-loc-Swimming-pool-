About Us

We specialise in the supply and installation of in-ground swimming pools, Spa pools (Hot tub or Jacuzzi), swimming pool equipment and general swimming pool supplies including chemicals within Nigeria.

Following the exposure of our team in Nigeria, we have found that our swimming pool design is the future of swimming pool construction due to its numerous advantages over the traditional concrete pool and other types of swimming pool construction in Nigeria.

With our level of professionalism, your pool is quality tested using agile approach and the materials are of best quality so the problem of crack or leakage is almost non-existent. In view of this, We ( K-loc Big & Partners Ltd) entered into an agreement with standardized pool material suppliers to ensure we regularly have quality materials.

K-loc is the most reputable pool company in Nigeria and have been manufacturing pools since 2016. We can modestly lay claim to being the first and best swimming pool company in Nigeria

.

We pride ourselves with delivering top quality products and services which helps us maintain an excellent relationship with all of our clients.

Our range of swimming pools designs and shapes cover almost every range of swimming pool that you may require.

With our success in the Nigerian swimming pool industry, K-loc Big & Partners Ltd has now recently expanded its operations to cover Nigeria and few Africa countries.
